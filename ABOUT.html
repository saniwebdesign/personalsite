Why I Chose BCOM ?

When I passed class 10 with 65%, I had three choices in front of me: science, arts, or commerce. I decided not to choose science because I was not very strong in that subject. I chose commerce because I wanted to become a chartered accountant.

In commerce, I studied subjects like accounting, tax law, and business studies, and I began to really enjoy them. During my higher school years, I developed a love for accounts and felt eager to learn more about it. Because of this growing interest, choosing to pursue a B.Com. was a natural next step for me.
